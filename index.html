Sensor

Monitor occupancy with ceiling-mounted sensors to gather data on how your spaces are being used. This information empowers you to make justified decisions backed by the data.

Heat mapping

Discover the power of real-time heat maps and gain valuable insights into how your spaces are being utilized. By visualizing foot traffic and occupancy patterns, you can optimize your building's layout for maximum efficiency. Unlock the full potential of your space by identifying high-traffic areas, reallocating resources, and making informed decisions that enhance both functionality and comfort.

Sustainable future

Transitioning from fossil fuels to green energy and reducing heating costs are key priorities in the Net Zero 2030 goals. Additionally, renting and heating underutilized spaces is a waste of resources and money. Therefore, optimizing the use of rental spaces is equally important to support both financial and environmental goals.

About us

We are a team of acoustic and AI experts applying the latest state-of-the-art AI research to provide insights into the built environments. Whether inefficient facility utilization, lack of in-depth knowledge on how your facilities are being used, or achieving Net Zero targets sound worrisome, our technology may be worth hearing out.

Our background is in developing noiseless stealth aircrafts and audiovisual camera algorithms in smart phones and self driving vehicles. Now, we are on a mission to bring our expertise into building designs which are far behind the technological advancements already applied to mobile, automotive, and aerospace industries.
