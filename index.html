Why does our solution matter?

Net Zero 2030 goal

The Energy Bill 2024 sets new regulations aimed at improving the UK's energy efficiency. Property owners must upgrade systems like insulation or heating, or face fines up to Â£150,000 or 20% of the rateable value of the property. [1][2]

In 2012, 87.2% of the energy consumed in the UK came from fossil fuels. In 2022, that number was 78.4%. Energy optimization
